Frequently Asked Questions

      

        

          Is ReadmeForge really free?
          
Yes — 100% free, no sign-up, no hidden fees. Everything runs in your browser.

        

        

          Where is my data stored?
          
Nowhere. All processing happens client-side. Your project info never leaves your device.

        

        

          Can I customize the generated markdown?
          
Absolutely. After generating, you can copy the markdown and edit it manually before committing.

        

        

          Do you support custom badges?
          
The current version supports 8+ Shields.io badges. Custom badge support is on the roadmap.

        

        

          Will my settings be remembered?
          
Yes — your form is saved in your browser's local storage so you can pick up where you left off.
